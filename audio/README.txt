The songs are set inside the website's editor, not here.

Open the site → tap the faint dot (bottom-left) → passcode admin123 →
scroll to the two Music sections → upload your MP3s → Publish.

There are TWO songs:
  • Main song   → plays the whole time she scrolls (commits as audio/song.mp3
                  or a uniquely-named audio/song-*.mp3). Starts on her first
                  tap/scroll (browsers block sound before that), and fades in.
  • Finale song → plays only when she reaches the bottom ("One year down,
                  a lifetime to go."), then the main song resumes if she
                  scrolls back up (commits as audio/song2-*.mp3).

The bottom-right button is a master on/off for the music.
You can still drop a file named exactly  song.mp3  here manually if you prefer.
If neither song is set, the music button just quietly disables itself.
(No finale song? The main song simply keeps playing at the bottom.)
